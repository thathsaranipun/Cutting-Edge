About

Inferno is Future Forest fire prediction application.It Predicts Forest fires based on global weather statistics.The Purpose of this is to save forests mainly and save propoerties and minimize the cost to extinguish these wild fires. Inferno is a mainly a research base application. The useres are able to see predicted forest fires and predicted weather statistics. And also to Pro-Usres and for the Organizations they are able to active Pre-Notification System from the Inferno.
