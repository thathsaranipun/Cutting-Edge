Humidity Distribution Graph

Relative humidity is a measure of the water vapour content of the air at a given temperature. The amount of moisture in the air is compared with the maximum amount that the air could contain at the same temperature and expressed as a percentage.

Wind Speed Distribution Graph

Knowledge of extreme wind speed distributions is essential for design of buildings and structures. Current methods based on statistical analysis of the maximum annual measured wind speeds are not adapted to regions which are prone to tropical cyclones. Indeed, part of the maximum annual winds are produced by statistically different populations, depending on whether they are or are not due to the passage of a cyclone; in addition, extreme winds due to tropical cyclones are relatively rare for a given site (approximatively 1 to 3 occurences in ten years), and, what is more, the strongest winds cannot be always measured.
